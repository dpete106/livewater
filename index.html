About

Livewater Farm & Dairy is a family owned and operated diversified dairy with an emphasis on regeneration, conservation and sustainability. Located in the hills of Westminster West Vermont since 1997 Livewater Farm produces the freshest grass-fed raw milk, cheeses and butter, as well as fresh eggs, meat, maple syrup, vegetables, canned goods and more.Everything is produced without the use of chemical fertilizers, hormones, antibiotics or pesticides. Easy to locate is the on- site farmstand where you can purchase all of our products as well as a bounty of other local goods including: breads, coffees and teas, herbs, mushrooms, tonics and more!
